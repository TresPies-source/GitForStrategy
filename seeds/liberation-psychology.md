# Seed: Liberation Psychology

**Confidence:** High — philosophical foundation

## Trigger
When barriers prevent people from contributing. When knowledge is hoarded instead of shared. When tools serve gatekeepers instead of users.

## Pattern
Remove barriers to contribution. Make knowledge commons. Design tools that democratize capability rather than concentrating it.

## Steps
1. Identify who can't currently contribute (and why)
2. Remove the structural barrier (not the person)
3. Make the default open, not closed
4. Measure by who CAN participate, not just who DOES

## Evidence
Open-sourcing the skill ecosystem (49 skills) enabled contributions from agents and humans who had no prior access to the methodology.

## Anti-Pattern
Expertise gatekeeping — requiring credentials to contribute. Or inverse: removing all structure (which creates different barriers).
