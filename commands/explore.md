# /explore

Explore before deciding. An alternative entry point to the pipeline when you need to understand something before you can even frame the tension.

## What This Does

1. Explore a project, codebase, or problem space systematically
2. Map philosophy, patterns, and architecture before committing
3. Identify entry points and potential tensions worth scouting

## How to Start

Ask the user: **"What are we exploring? A codebase, a project, a problem space, or a research topic?"**

Then route to the appropriate skill:
- **New project/codebase:** Use project-exploration from `skills/explore/project-exploration/SKILL.md`
- **Research topic:** Use research-modes from `skills/explore/research-modes/SKILL.md`
- **Strategic tension:** Redirect to `/scout`

## Output

Exploration notes that identify tensions worth scouting. Natural lead-in to `/scout`.
